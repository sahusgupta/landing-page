About Me

Hello! I’m Sahus Gupta, a junior at Obra D. Tompkins High School in Katy, TX, pursuing a STEM endorsement. My coursework spans AP Computer Science A, Game Development, CSIII Independent Study, Multivariable Calculus, and more.

I’ve co-founded a test-proctoring startup, built an AI-based platform for college essay guidance, and created multiple software solutions that bridge gaps in education and promote accessibility. I love using Python, Java, Dart (Flutter), Golang, and React.js to solve real-world problems.

Apart from coding, you can find me coaching tennis, reading math textbooks at odd hours, playing Brawl Stars or VALORANT, and dabbling with my “billion-dollar ideas.” My experiences, from the NASA Space Apps Challenge to PlatoAI, have shaped me into someone who genuinely believes that technology and empathy can transform lives globally.
